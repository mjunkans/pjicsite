About Us
With 20+ years of experience, PJ-IC brings growth-oriented strategies, local expertise, global connections and high-level resources to facilitate projects of considerable scale, intricacy and capability.

Collaborating with local and national governments, business and communities with hands-on facilitation and strategic guidance, we design, finance, and execute projects for all parties to realize true growth and sustainability.
Why We’re Here

We don’t just start with WHY:
It goes before, during and Beyond our delivery.

Our mission is to bring both prosperity and sustainability on a local and national scale. We believe growth-oriented business and sustainability can co-exist when we’re willing to re-define profit and prosperity to create lasting value and increased quality of life at every opportunity. As such, we strive diligently to hand over a set of keys to those who seek the same.

Rather than business as power – it’s business that empowers.
